Recently, I’ve been receiving messages from the universe—or *Gus*, a handy acronym for God, Universe, and Source, as I’ve heard some people call it.

For as long as I can remember, the Universe and I have been in conversation. Recently, though, we’ve been communicating less and less—like a friendship separated by distance that slowly devolves from long phone conversations into nothing more than “likes” on Instagram.

It wasn’t that the messages were subtle—it was that I was. Or maybe more accurately, I was disconnected. The messages were likely always there… until one day, I was ready to pick up.
